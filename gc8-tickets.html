The App

GC8-Tickets, short for GUSAC Carnival 8.0 Tickets, is an Android app developed to improve and ease the registration procedure for the event. The app is simple to use and secure as it helps authenticate user tickets on the day of the event.

The app was used by GUSAC Carnival registrar team to register, verify & log details of all 1800+ attendees of the event.

Scan Tickets!

The app is super smooth to use! Simply hit the scan button on the bottom right and scan the QR on the GUSAC Carnival 8.0 ticket.

The app will scan the ticket within few milliseconds and fetch its details.

Register

The app allows registering ticket with the user details. These details are safely stored in a database.

The app also allows unregistering tickets on user's request as per refund policy.

Verify & Log Attendees

The app shows registration information & helps to authenticate tickets at the time of event entry.

The team at the entrance/exit can also log/record check-in and check-out times of attendees.
